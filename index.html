Who We Are

Lorubae Comprehensive School rises from the sun-baked earth of Samburu East – a land of sweeping savannahs, ancient acacia trees, and skies that stretch without apology to the horizon. In a region where the wind carries the dust of centuries and rain is a gift rather than a guarantee, this school stands as a deliberate act of hope. Here, in one of Kenya's most arid and remote landscapes, knowledge is not merely a motto – it is a lifeline. We serve both day and boarding students with a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.
