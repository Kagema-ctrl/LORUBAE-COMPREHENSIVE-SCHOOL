Who We Are

Lorubae Comprehensive School rises from the sun-baked earth of Samburu East – a land of sweeping savannahs, ancient acacia trees, and skies that stretch without apology to the horizon. In a region where the wind carries the dust of centuries and rain is a gift rather than a guarantee, this school stands as a deliberate act of hope. Here, in one of Kenya's most arid and remote landscapes, knowledge is not merely a motto – it is a lifeline. We serve both day and boarding students with a commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

Principal's Welcome

Moses Kagema Kooro

Proverbs 24:27 reminds us: "Prepare your outside work, Make it fit for yourself in the field; And afterward build your house." At Lorubae, we live by this principle, preparing our students to cultivate knowledge, integrity, and purpose in the broader world before they build their own futures. Education is not a destination; it is the foundation upon which lives of meaning, dignity, and service are built. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community engagement ensures that every student who leaves these gates carries with them the tools, wisdom, and confidence to create lasting change; not just for themselves, but for Samburu East and beyond.

"Prepare your outside work, Make it fit for yourself in the field; And afterward build your house."

— Proverbs 24:27

Before we can build great futures, we must first prepare the ground. At Lorubae, education is that preparation.
